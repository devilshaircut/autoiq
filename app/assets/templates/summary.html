Tips for Selling Your Car

  • Price to Sell

    This is the easiest thing for you to do. You’re on AutoID where we will provide you with a realistic price recommendation. After entering your specifics above, you’ll see the Edmunds True Market Private Party value of your vehicle.

  • Create an Effective Listing

    AutoID provides you with rich assets for you to create a great vehicle listing. We provide you with vehicle details, stock photos and pricing recommendations to get you started. Add your own custom description, additional photos and pricing adjustments to further enhance your listing.

  • New Floor Mats

    This is one of the cheapest purchases you can make to make a first impression. Hopping into the interior of a car that has brand new floor mats, immediately makes the rest of the car feel cleaner. This little upgrade will pay off in spades.

  • Get Your Car Serviced

    If your car needs service, go to the service shop before listing it. The number one thing that turns off buyers are obvious bodywork, check engine lights, or routine maintenance not completed when they meet you for the walkaround. If you’re not sure if you need service, get it professionally inspected.
